The World Is Changing

Shift 1

Search → Chat

58.5% of Google searches now end in zero clicks. Gartner predicts 25% decline in traditional search by 2026.

Source: SparkToro/Datos 2024, Gartner 2024

Shift 2

Websites → On-the-fly UI

MCP protocol adopted by VS Code, Claude, ChatGPT. 70% of developers aware of MCP in just 9 months.

Source: Postman State of API 2025

Shift 3

Portals → Embedded

Users expect platforms to come to them — in their IDE, their chat, their workflow. Not the other way around.

82% of orgs are now API-first (Postman 2025)

The era of classic portals is at risk. Platforms that don't externalize their capabilities risk becoming invisible.
