Executive Summary

Three Shifts Are Reshaping How People Work

Search → Chat

Users ask AI instead of clicking links. AI summaries replace traditional browsing.

58.5% of Google searches end in zero clicks

Static UIs → Agent UIs

AI agents generate on-the-fly interfaces. Users interact through tools like MCP Apps.

70% of API developers are MCP-aware

Portals → Embedded

Users expect platforms to come to them — in their IDE, chat, or CI/CD pipeline.

82% of orgs follow API-first strategies

What This Means for Fabric

Platforms that only live in a browser portal risk becoming invisible. Fabric must be embeddable, invocable, and composable — wherever users and agents work.
